What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables computers and machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and recognizing images. Key Points Full Form: Artificial Intelligence Introduced by: John McCarthy Year: 1956 Goal: To create intelligent systems that can learn, think, and make decisions. Features of AI Learns from data. Solves complex problems. Recognizes speech and images.
