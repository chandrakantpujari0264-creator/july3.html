What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI is a type of Artificial Intelligence that can plan, make decisions, and perform tasks independently to achieve a specific goal. Unlike traditional AI, which mainly responds to user commands, Agentic AI can take multiple steps, adapt to changes, and complete complex tasks with limited human intervention. Meaning: AI systems that act as autonomous agents. Purpose: To achieve goals by planning, reasoning, and taking actions. Works by: Observing, planning, executing tasks, and learning from results. Features of Agentic AI Autonomous decision-making Goal-oriented behavior. Planning and reasoning. Learns from feedback. Can perform multiple-step tasks. Interacts with users and software tools.
